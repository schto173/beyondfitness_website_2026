FREE TALKS SCHEDULE

SATURDAY 27 June
  • 13:00 BEYOND STRETCHING A Somatic Approach to Nervous System Regulation and Chronic Tension Release · Iryna Sagaidak PhD, Somatic Therapist and Feldenkrais Practitioner

    What you will learn:

    • Why chronic tension, persistent stress symptoms, and pain often refuse to go away despite exercise, stretching, and physiotherapy — and what the nervous system has to do with it.
    • How the brain and nervous system learn to hold tension as a habit, an introduction to neuroplastic symptoms, and what somatic approaches can do that conventional methods can't.
    • A live experience of somatic movement (Feldenkrais Method): how gentle, mindful movement can shift the nervous system out of a guarded state and toward ease.
    • Practical tools to take home: simple somatic practices to release habitual tension patterns and build lasting regulation from the inside out.
  • 14:00 PERSONAL TRAINING BEYOND STEREOTYPES Marco Pezzetti · Personal trainer

    About this talk:

    Personal training is often misunderstood as a service for the wealthy delivered by social media fitness icons. In reality, it is an accessible profession made up of diverse individuals who, like many self-employed professionals in Luxembourg, navigate both opportunities and challenges while helping others achieve their goals.

    • How to make personal training accessible and relevant to diverse clients.
    • Practical approaches to building trust and long-term client results.
    • Real-world examples of overcoming stereotypes and delivering value.
  • 15:00 CM ACTIVE LIFE Empower Women Through Fitness, Move & Active Lifestyle · Caroline Manuelle (English) · Fitness coach & Wellness

    What you will learn:

    • How to self-transform through sports and movement.
    • Why sports = health and the impact of regular activity on mental wellbeing.
    • The importance of having the right coach and how to find or become one.
    • How to overcome mental challenges through sport and physical activity.
  • 16:00 BEYOND LONGEVITY: BIOHACKING YOUR HEALTHSPAN Manlio Lo Giudice · Founder of MobiGym, Biohacker, Human Potential Development Coach

    What you will learn:

    • Why the real goal is not just to live longer, but to stay strong, energetic, mobile, and mentally sharp for longer.
    • How simple biohacking tools — movement, strength training, recovery, breath, light, sleep, and nutrition — can support healthspan.
    • Why muscle, cardiovascular fitness, and brain health are key pillars of longevity.
    • Practical, science-based habits people can start applying immediately to improve energy, resilience, and long-term wellbeing.
  • 17:00 STRENGTH TRAINING 30+ Transform Your Health & Wellbeing · Andreea Marin · Personal Trainer - Nutritional Advisor

    What you will learn:

    • Why strength training becomes essential after 30 and how it prevents muscle loss, boosts metabolism, and strengthens bones.
    • Real client transformations — hear from three women who changed their lives through strength training.
    • Myth-busting session: addressing common misconceptions about weight training, dieting, and starting fitness later in life.
  • 18:00 COMO VIVER EM ALTA PERFORMANCE Caroline Manuelle (Português) · Fitness coach e Saúde

    About this talk:

    O poder da transformação através do desporto. Actividade física = Saúde. Importância de ter um coach adequado às suas necessidades. Como superar problemas psicológicos através do desporto.

    • Como a atividade física transforma a vida.
    • Estratégias práticas para escolher um coach adequado.
    • Técnicas para ultrapassar desafios psicológicos através do movimento.

SUNDAY 28 June
  • 13:00 REGULATION OF NERVOUS SYSTEM THROUGH PILATES PRACTICE Iro Peklari-Petrou

    About this talk:

    ⁠how breathing works in pilates, the anatomy and mechanics of the breath (which muscles are being used, what happens when we are stressed and how it impacts our breath)

    • The principles of pilates
    • How through pilates we can regulate our nervous system
    • A few words about the nervous system
    • The outcome: why we want to do regulate the nervous system through pilates?
  • 14:00 BIOHACKING ABUNDANCE — COACH PSYCHOLOGIST Rewiring the Code of Your Beliefs · Miriam Vetrano · Psychologist

    About this workshop:

    In this interactive workshop, you’ll explore how your beliefs shape the way you perceive opportunities, make decisions, and create results in your life. You will discover how your brain is constantly filtering what you notice, what you may be unconsciously overlooking, and what you believe is possible for you.

    • How beliefs filter perception and behaviour.
    • Practical exercises to start rewiring limiting beliefs.
    • Tools to notice and expand what you believe is possible.
  • 15:00 KOUKSUNDO Traditional Korean Taoist Mind-Body Practice · Eglė Kumžaitė

    About this class:

    A short introductory class of Kouksundo practice that includes physical exercises regulating the energy flows in the body, self-acupressure stimulating the functioning of the organs, and breathing meditation with movement calming down the mind. Kouksundo has a healing effect, strengthens the body, brings inner peace and is accessible to everyone.

    • Principles and basic exercises of Kouksundo.
    • Self-acupressure and breathing practices.
    • Who benefits most and how to continue practice safely.
  • 16:00 MODERN POSTURE & TECH NECK How Breathing, Movement and Clinical Pilates Can Support the Body · Martina Di Modica · Pilates Teacher, Sports Scientist & Massage Therapist

    About this talk:

    How modern lifestyle habits and screen use affect posture, breathing, and neck tension. The connection between breathing mechanics, stress, and cervical/lower back discomfort. How Clinical Pilates, physiotherapy, and massage therapy can help improve movement quality and reduce chronic tension. Simple breathing and posture awareness techniques participants can immediately apply in daily life.

    • Quick posture checks and daily corrections.
    • Breathing patterns to reduce neck and upper-back tension.
    • Short corrective sequences to integrate into your routine.
